About Us

Zemer Orchestra is the soul of Baltimore's music. From weddings to concerts to dinners to bar mitzvahs, our live band guarantees to uplift and enhance your event. Zemer's versatility, flexibility, and genuine care makes the whole experience pleasant and easy.

Zemer Orchestra began as "Zemer Achim" back in the 80's in Silver Spring, MD - a band of brothers and friends bringing their heart, soul, and talent to their community. Though many decades have passed since, Zemer has only grown in its vast array of musicians and its passion to bring joy to your event.

Zemer Orchestra offers a wide range of instruments:

  • Piano
  • Violin
  • Flute
  • Saxophone
  • Cello
  • Clarinet
  • Harp
  • Drums
  • Acoustic Guitar
  • Electric Guitar
  • Trumpet
  • Trombone
  • And so much more!
